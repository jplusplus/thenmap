ThenShapes
==========

These are the shape files for thenmap (https://github.com/rotsee/thenmap)

They are partly based on CSHAPES by Nils B. Weidmann, Doreen Kuse, Kristian Skrede Gleditsch, especially from 1975 and on

Feature

Dataset format
--------------

ESRI shapefile, WGS84 reference system, longitude/latitude coordinates.
